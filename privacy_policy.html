WebTasker Privacy Policy

Introduction

WebTasker is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection and Use

WebTasker does not collect or store any personal information on external servers. All data, including your tasks, are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.

  • Tasks: Your tasks are stored locally and are associated with the URLs of the websites you visit.

Data Access and Permissions

WebTasker requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • storage: This permission is used to save your tasks locally in your browser.
  • tabs: This enables the extension to get information about the currently active tab for task management.

Data Sharing and Transfer

WebTasker does not share, sell, rent, or trade any information with third parties. All your data remains on your local device.

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. By storing all data locally, we minimize the risk of unauthorized access. However, please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

Your Rights

As all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your information. You can view, edit, or delete your data at any time through the extension interface or by clearing your browser data.

Contact Us
