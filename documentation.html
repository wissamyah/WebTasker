WebTasker Privacy Policy

Purpose of WebTasker

WebTasker is a simple Chrome extension designed to help users create and manage to-do lists directly within their browser. It aims to boost productivity by allowing users to organize tasks based on the websites they visit, making task management more intuitive and efficient.

Key Features

Website-Specific Task Lists

WebTasker creates unique task lists for each website you visit. This allows you to manage tasks specific to different contexts, such as work projects, personal errands, or hobby-related activities.

Easy Task Management

Add, complete, and delete tasks with simple clicks. Tasks are automatically saved and synced across your Chrome browser, ensuring your lists are always up-to-date.

How to Use WebTasker

  1. Adding Tasks: Click on the WebTasker icon, type your task in the input field, and click "Add Task" or press Enter.
  2. Completing Tasks: Click on a task to mark it as complete. Click again to mark it as incomplete.
  3. Deleting Tasks: Click the "×" button next to a task to remove it from your list.

Permissions Used

  • storage: Used to save your tasks locally in your browser.
  • tabs: Enables the extension to get information about the currently active tab.

Privacy and Data Usage

WebTasker respects your privacy. All your task data is stored locally in your browser. The extension does not send any personal data to external servers. For more detailed information, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
